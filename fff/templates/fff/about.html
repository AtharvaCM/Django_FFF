About Company Page


    FreshFromFarm is a portal for farmers to sell their goods directly to the customers from which the farmers will get a justifiable amount for their products.


     For many years in India we keep hearing 'farmer commited suicide' for various reasons, one of which is, not getting fair price for the goods they are producing. We shop at the market or a mart where we need to pay a significant amount for our vegetables. But most oftenly this amount contains a cut for the mediators who bring you the vegetables or the goods from the farmers. This is okay if they are paying enough to the producers for all the work they put in but this is not the case. ergo, we at FreshFromFarm have taken the initiative to provide a platform for the 'Kisan'(s) of the India to sell their products for a just price and with no greedy mediators.
